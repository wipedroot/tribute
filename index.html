Software developer, cryptologist

  • 1956 Born in Coalinga, California, US
  • 1979 Graduated from California Institute of Technology with a BS in Engineering
  • 1990 Ran two anonymous emailers while being a founding member of the Cypherpunk movement
  • 2004 Created the first reusable proof of work system, a method that would later be incorporated in the Bitcoin software
  • 2009 Hal Finney became the recipient of the first transaction on the Bitcoin network
  • 2011 Retired from the PGP Corporation, where he worked along with its founder Phil Zimmerman
  • 2013 Hal Finney announced that he was effectively paralyzed, due to him suffering from ALS
  • 2014 As a result of ALS complications, Hal Finney died in Phoenix, Arizona

Hal Finney remains in a cryopreserved state at Alcor Life Extension Foundation
